About me

My name is Jared Castaneda! I'm 22 years old and currently a junior at California State University, Fullerton.

My goal is to provide excellent work alongside a great attitude, willingness to work with a team, new ideas, creativity, optimistic work habits, and more to a professional environment. There's plenty of opportunities for me to apply these to my current school life, so I think the transition to a workspace would be very accessible to me.

I chose to major in computer science because I felt it suited what I could contribute to in the future. I also felt very comfortable because many of my friends, family, and peers all were working with computer science or web design. This was one of the early stepping stones for me to take the journey into computer science, and I haven't looked back since.

When I'm not programming, studying, or brainstorming new project ideas, my life is never boring. I can be seen at the gym, at a concert, practicing film photography, playing games with my friends, going on hikes, or studying film history in my free time.
